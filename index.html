About me

I am a Master's student at IMPA. I have been interested in Algebra for years, but after attending some amusing talks I have been feeling increasingly attracted to Probability.

I got my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from the University of São Paulo in 2021. There I did a research project on the representation theory of associative algebras advised by Prof. Kostiantyn Iusenko. I also got involved in the A5 Group, a group formed by Math students that organized seminars and outreach events, and in the organization of the first EPPM, a regional conference for Math graduate students.

In 2022, I started my Master's at IMPA with funding from CAPES, a federal agency in Brazil. I am a member of the board of directors of IMPA's Graduate Student Association.

From 2017 to the end of 2019, I worked part-time teaching Mathematics for competitions to children and teenagers.

You can find more professional information about me in my CV.

I have a life outside of Mathematics as well.

My boyfriend is also a mathematician.
