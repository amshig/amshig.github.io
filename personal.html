About me (non-mathematical)

I moved to Rio de Janeiro in March 2022 to study at IMPA. Since then, I've been adapting to the carioca lifestyle and liberating myself from the frenzy that is natural to anyone from São Paulo (as much as I love that city). I really enjoy being able to go to the beach when I have a few hours to spare, just to look at the sea and relax a bit — for free!

I also got a new hobbie here, which is dancing forró! This is a very fun Brazilian dance that, in my opinion, everyone should try. The weekly forró classes help to relieve the stress from my Master's, because I get so concentrated on following my partner's lead that I have no room to think about anything else. Knowing this dance has also made parties a lot more fun.

Aside from these recent habits, I have some older hobbies too. Drawing has always been my favorite, though I haven't had a lot of time for it lately. For now, I content myself with consumming other people's drawings by reading manga and digital comics.

Once upon a time, I used to play softball. I hope to get back to it some day.

About my name

I am a Japanese descendant from Brazil. Adriana and Mayumi are given names, and Shiguihara is my family name. The gui is pronounced as the gee in geek (not as gu + i). It is written in this way (with an u) because that is how we write this sound in Portuguese. The other syllables are pronounced as expected from a Japanese word.
